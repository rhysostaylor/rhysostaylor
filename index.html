Rhys Taylor

Rhys is a British/New Zealand businessperson living in Singapore. He has held executive positions in the financial services and marketing industries

In 2012 he was a member of the Management Team responsible for Marketing when Acclipse was acquired by Wolters Kluwer; Acclipse developed tax workflow and content management SaaS products

Later Rhys worked across Asia Pacific as a consultant at Wunderman-Bienalto -- a digital transformation firm acquired by WPP plc. And in 2020 he became Managing Director at Dentsu Inc., responsible for the B2B Strategy and Creative Services practice

Most recently Rhys returned to WPP in 2023 as Managing Director at VML -- a $2B+ brand and service design agency with 15,000 employees worldwide
